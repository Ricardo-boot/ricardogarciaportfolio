Milestone 1

Purpose

My personal website will be a professional portfolio that will aid in developing my professional career as a web developer, programmer, and all around IT specialist. It will serve as a base for showcasing and directing to every project that I decide to create and work on from here on out as well as allowing any interested employers or teams to contact me. This project will also serve as a grounds for displaying my evolving ability through the constant updates and features that I implement to it as I learn more and more skills.

Audience

My target audience for this project will be both professional and non-professional individuals with the intention of wanting me to colloaborate with them. Whether employers reaching out for a job opportunity, peers seeking to find a team member for their personal project, or an inquiring individual who wishes to learn about web development themselves, I will let this portfolio serve as a gateway to any potential avenue of developing my web development abilities.

Goals

  • To get the attention of employers and potential clients.
  • To recieve job offers and secure a decent paying job for myself in the IT/CompSci field.
  • To recieve offers to collaborate on projects on that other web developers are working on.
  • To serve as an archive of every project that I make from here on out.
  • To secure a reputation of a consummate web developer who produces quality work.

Persona

Name: Dylan May

Age: 26

Profession: Self employed web developer that has been running his business with only himself and a few other devs to help him manage the work load. Works mainly from home in a specially furnished room that he has made into his office. His work consists of developing clean websites with great functionality for clients that range from small business start ups to big commercial chains.

Technology: Custom built PC, 1,400 dollars worth of the some of the best components that have just released out on the market. Uses his desktop for work, simple multitasking programs, high quality gaming, and video editing and rendering. Owns a Rasberry Pi for use with tinkering and learning about blockchain technology and hooking into the ethereum network. Owns a Samsung Galaxy A10e as its a solid price and he enjoys customizing his phone as much as he wants, running emulators and changing the display and icons on everything as well as installing custom widgets that he developed.

Attitudes and Behaviors: Pretty laid back, but very serious about the quality of his work and maintaining a bit of punctuality. Always has work that needs to be doing as he is good at socializing which allows him to network and connect with potential client either directly or indirectly. Finds himself stressed out at times due to the size of the work load and requires a few more hands to help with all his clients. Uses social media mainly for professional tasks and the occasional contact to get together with friends, as he tries to limit it as he sees it as a time sink for anything not related to work or news. Wants to really get to know a candidate first, and will give them a chance of proving themselves by working for a week or two first so that he can get a deeper understanding of the type of person he is hiring, as he knows that quickly gleaning at candidates wont really show him what kind of individual he is hiring.

Frustrations and Needs: Dylan needs an employee who understands the tools that he is working with as well as all the encompassing technology and software so that he doesn't need to constantly teach them everything. He is looking for somebody who is excited about working as a web developer and spends a lot of their time researching the subject and always learning new skills in order to stay relevant in the field. Dylan puts priority on skill and knowledge rather than if someone has a "positive" personality, though he still keeps courteousness in mind. Dylans only frustrations are that he feels that he doesn't have enough time to properly get to know someone before hiring them.

Goals: Dylan wants and expects to see what my capabilities are through looking at my portfolio. He wants to see that the work is of good quality and that everything functions properly and has no bugs. Through my porfolio he expects to see how creative my ideas for websites are and how well I am able to execute that idea.

User-Story: As a web developer, I need to find an individual who takes web development seriously, has a passion for it and spends a good portion of his free time studying it and constantly expanding his pool of knowledge in order to bring this with him to the company.
